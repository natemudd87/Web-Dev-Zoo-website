BrushWind Zoo

Welcome to BrushWind Zoo! Established in 2002. We are home to a wide variety of animals.
We hope that you and your family come and visit us and experience the best zoo in the world.
We also have a delicious restaurant and unlimited free soft drinks! 70% of our animals were rescued
from the exotic pet trade or from poor living conditions in circuses or other zoos.
So you can always feel good about visiting and supporting our zoo
because we put the well being of our animals above making money.

Conservation Efforts

Our zoo engages in many conservation activities we work with animal sanctuaries
to rescue neglected animals from poor living conditions and rehabilitate them in hopes of
eventually returning them to the wild. We encourage you to donate to our efforts if you are able. Thank you for your support!
